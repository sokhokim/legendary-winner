Admin

I support the behind-the-scenes work that helps both clients and clinicians have a smooth, stress-free experience. About Sokho

Hi, I’m Sokho.

I support onboarding, billing, insurance coordination, website management, and general administrative and technical needs for both clients and the team.

With a background in ministry, I value supporting a faith-informed environment when it is meaningful and appropriate.

Core Values:

  • Dependability

My Style

I take a detail-oriented and organized approach, with a preference for clear, thoughtful communication through email. I strive to be responsive, thorough, and consistent in supporting both clients and the team.

Training

Background

My background includes experience in ministry, healthcare, and business operations. I have served as a pastor in a local church, served as a chaplain at the National Institutes of Health, and managed operations as a branch manager in the mortgage industry. These roles have strengthened my ability to support individuals with care while also managing complex systems and logistics. I bring a balanced perspective of compassion, structure, and problem-solving to my role.

Outside of Work
