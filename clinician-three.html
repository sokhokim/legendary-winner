Amber Kirkpatrick

Amber Kirkpatrick, LMSWProfile Ready

Amber is a Licensed Clinical Social Work Associate in North Carolina who supports adolescents and adults navigating anxiety, depression, and trauma. She offers a warm, relaxed, and authentic approach, meeting clients where they are and incorporating a faith-based perspective when desired.

About Amber

Hi, I am Amber I am a licensed clinical social work associate in NC. First, I am a human. I understand how scary it can be getting stuck in the cycle of anxiety or trauma. I also understand how sometimes we can feel like no one else truly understands what you are going through. It can be scary to feel panic rob your body's ability to think or take a breath while still trying to appear that everything is okay. I believe that each person has the ability to overcome their past trauma, anxiety and depression with the proper tools. I treat adolescents through adults with histories of trauma, depression and anxiety. I am happy to offer a faith-based approach for anyone that would be interested. Core values- My top 3 values when working with people are authenticity, reliability and autonomy. My style is more relaxed and meeting people where they are at that time. Training-I earned my MSW through East Tennessee State university. I have had continuing training in Acceptance, commitment therapy (ACT), Cognitive Behavioral therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavioral therapy (DBT) and Solution Focused Brief therapy (SFBT). My background-I have prior experience working with teens navigating the foster care system. I have worked in government economic services and community mental health serving low income families while gaining insight into how a person's environment can impact mental health. I have recently been working as a social worker and counselor in an elementary level school. Interest-When I am not working I enjoy spending time with my family and my dogs going to the lake. I am an avid animal lover and enjoy them. I also enjoy unwinding with crafts of any kind and welcome other crafters bringing their love for crafting into session.
