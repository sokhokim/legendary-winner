Clinician Profile

Amber Kirkpatrick, LCSW-A

NC Telehealth

Amber is a Licensed Clinical Social Work Associate in North Carolina who supports adolescents and adults navigating anxiety, depression, and trauma. She offers a warm, relaxed, and authentic approach, meeting clients where they are and incorporating a faith-based perspective when desired.

About Amber

Hi, I’m Amber.

I understand how overwhelming it can feel to be stuck in cycles of anxiety or trauma, and how isolating it can be when it seems like no one truly understands what you’re going through. Experiencing panic can feel like it takes over your body—making it difficult to think clearly or even catch your breath—while still trying to appear as though everything is okay on the outside.

I believe that every person has the capacity to heal and grow beyond past trauma, anxiety, and depression when given the right tools and support. I strive to create a safe, supportive space where you can feel understood and begin to move forward.

For those who are interested, I am happy to incorporate a Christian faith-based approach into our work together.

Core Values: Authenticity. Reliability. Autonomy.

My Style

My approach is relaxed, collaborative, and centered on meeting you where you are. I aim to create a space where you feel comfortable, supported, and understood at every stage of your journey.

Training

  • Master of Social Work (MSW), East Tennessee State University
  • Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT) – Basic Skills
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Solution-Focused Brief Therapy (SFBT)

Background

Amber has experience working with teens navigating the foster care system, as well as in government economic services and community mental health settings serving low-income families. These experiences have provided valuable insight into how environmental factors impact mental health.

Most recently, she has worked as a social worker and counselor in an elementary school setting.

Outside of Therapy

Outside of work, Amber enjoys spending time with her family and her dogs, especially going to the lake. She is an avid animal lover and enjoys creative activities and crafts. Clients who enjoy crafting are welcome to incorporate that into sessions if it feels helpful.

Licensure & Availability

  • Licensed in North Carolina
  • Telehealth appointments available
