Clinician Profile

Gabriel Smith, LMSW

MD Telehealth

Gabriel Smith is a Licensed Master Social Worker (serving in Maryland) who supports adolescents and adults navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, and substance use. He offers a warm, collaborative approach and can incorporate a Christian faith-based perspective for those who desire it.

About Gabriel

Hi, I’m Gabriel.

I understand how overwhelming it can feel to carry anxiety, depression, grief, or past trauma without knowing how to move forward. You may feel stuck in patterns that seem impossible to break—whether that’s constant worry, emotional numbness, or turning to unhealthy coping strategies.

It can be exhausting trying to hold everything together while feeling like you’re falling apart inside. I believe that with the right support, healing is possible, and you don’t have to walk through it alone.

I work with adolescents through adults navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, substance use, and other mental health challenges. I also offer a faith-based perspective for those who desire to incorporate their faith into the counseling process.

Core Values:

  • Genuineness
  • Compassion
  • Integrity

My Style

My approach is warm, authentic, and collaborative. I aim to create a safe space where you can be honest, feel understood, and work toward meaningful change at your own pace—whether that means making big life changes or understanding how small shifts can have a powerful impact.

Training

  • Licensed Master Social Worker (LMSW)
  • Assessment for Substance Abuse
  • Cognitive behavioral Therapy
  • Mindfulness-based Therapy
  • Solution-Focused Therapy

Background

I have over a year of experience working as an outpatient counselor in a substance use treatment setting, helping individuals navigate recovery, mental health concerns, and life transitions.

Outside of Therapy

Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with family, growing in faith, and playing golf, basketball, and going to the gym.

Licensure & Availability

  • Licensed in Maryland
  • Telehealth appointments available
