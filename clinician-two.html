Clinician Profile

Gabriel Smith, LMSW

MDTelehealth

Gabriel Smith is a Licensed Master Social Worker who supports adolescents and adults navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, and substance use. He offers a warm, collaborative approach and can incorporate a Christian faith-based perspective for those who desire it.

About Gabriel

Hi, Hi, I’m Gabriel Smith. I understand how overwhelming it can feel to carry anxiety, depression, grief, or past trauma and not knowing how to move forward. You may feel stuck in patterns that seem impossible to break, whether that’s constant worry, emotional numbness, or turning to wrong directions just to cope. It can be exhausting trying to hold everything together while feeling like you’re falling apart inside. I believe that with the right support, healing is possible and you don’t have to walk through it alone. I work with adolescents through adults navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, grief, substance use, and other mental health challenges. I also offer a faith-based perspective for those who desire to incorporate their faith into the counseling process. Core Values: Genuineness. Compassion. Integrity. My Style: My approach is warm, authentic, and collaborative, creating a safe space where you can be honest, feel understood, and work toward meaningful change at your own pace. Whether it is making big changes or understanding the impact little changes can have on our lives. Training: Licensed Master of Social Work (LMSW) Assessment for Substance Abuse Background: I have over a year of experience working as an outpatient counselor in a substance use treatment setting, helping individuals navigate recovery, mental health concerns, and life Transitions. Personal Interests: Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my family, growing in my faith, and I enjoy playing golf, basketball, and going to the gym.
