Services

Telehealth therapy designed to be clear and accessible.

All services are offered via secure video sessions. Services are not currently offered by phone or in person.

How Services Work

  • HIPAA-compliant video platform
  • Typically 55-minute sessions
  • Clients must be located in a state where the clinician is licensed
  • Weekly sessions are generally recommended

Attendance Expectations

Consistent attendance supports progress. Sessions are typically held weekly unless otherwise agreed upon. Inconsistent attendance may impact outcomes and could result in discontinuation of services.

Session Types

  • Individual Therapy: ~55 minutes
  • Family Sessions: 30–50 minutes
  • Recurring appointments to support consistency
  • Parent involvement is expected for minors

Structured & Integrative Care

We utilize structured, evidence-based modalities alongside integrative approaches tailored to each client.

Structured Modalities: EMDR, TF-CBT, DBT

Integrative Approaches: CBT, Motivational Interviewing, ACT, Somatic, Solution-Focused, Mindfulness-Based, Emotion-Focused

Optional: Faith-integrated therapy upon request

Treatment Process: What to Expect

  1. Consultation: Brief call to determine fit
  2. Schedule Intake: First full session is booked
  3. Insurance/Payment: Verified prior to start
  4. Complete Paperwork: Due 24 hours before intake
  5. Diagnostic Evaluation: First 1–2 sessions
  6. Treatment Planning: Sessions 3–4
  7. Ongoing Therapy: Typically weekly sessions
  8. Progress Monitoring: Ongoing assessment
  9. Plan Review: Every 4–6 months
  10. Discharge: Gradual reduction when goals met
  11. Self-Pay Option: Continue if desired
