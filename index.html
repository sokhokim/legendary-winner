About Us

A place to heal. A team that truly cares.

Rest & We are a team of therapists supporting adolescents and adults navigating anxiety, trauma, depression, and life transitions. Using evidence-based approaches such as EMDR, CBT, and DBT-informed care—with optional faith integration—we support you in restoring balance and moving toward greater peace and purpose.

Our shared mission is to create a warm, inclusive space where you can feel safe to explore, heal, and grow.
