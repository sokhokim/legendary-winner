Ready to take the next step?

Once you’re ready to schedule your free 15-minute consultation, click the “Schedule Consultation” button. If you have any difficulty booking, you’re welcome to reach out directly to our admin team at admin@restrestoretherapy.com for assistance. After scheduling, you’ll receive an email with a few brief forms to complete at least 24 hours prior to your consultation. These forms, along with the consultation, help us determine whether one of our providers’ skills, style, and availability are a good fit for your needs.

Payment Options

    Insurance accepted The following plans are accepted for North Carolina and Maryland clients: Blue Cross Blue Shield, BCBS Federal Plans, Carefirst Self-Pay Cash pay: $150 per 55-minute session Diagnostic Evaluation Sessions: $160 per 55-minute session Out of Network Benefits We can provide superbills and provide support submitting superbills directly to the payer. This allows patients to see providers who are not credentialed as in-network with their insurance company. Out-of-Pocket Fees Certain services are not covered by insurance and will be charged directly to the card on file at the time of service. These may include: Late cancellations and no-shows Treatment summaries or documentation requests Paperwork or form completion Coordination of care with other providers (at your request) Court-related requests or services These fees cannot be billed to insurance and are outlined in detail in our intake paperwork. Insurance & Benefits Responsibility As a courtesy, our team will verify your insurance benefits prior to starting services. However, please note that it is ultimately your responsibility to understand your coverage. We strongly encourage all clients to contact their insurance provider directly to confirm benefits, including: Deductibles Copays or coinsurance Session limits or authorization requirements Clients are responsible for payment of any services not covered by insurance. In some cases, initial eligibility checks may indicate that services are in-network, but later processing may determine that they are out-of-network. Additionally, if your insurance plan is not based in the state where you reside, services may be considered out-of-network. Deductibles If your plan includes a deductible, you will be responsible for the full session fee (based on your clinician’s contracted insurance rate) until your insurance company confirms that your deductible has been met.
